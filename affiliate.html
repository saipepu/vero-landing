Frequently Asked Questions

How much do I earn per referral?

£5 per successful referral. A referral is "successful" when your friend signs up with your code and claims their first reward within 14 days of signing up.

Is there a cap on how much I can earn?

No cap. Refer as many friends as you like and earn £5 for each successful conversion.

How long does payment take?

Payouts are reviewed manually and paid within 7 business days of a successful conversion. We'll contact you via your registered email to arrange bank transfer details.

What does my friend get?

Your friend receives bonus XP and tokens when they sign up with your referral code — giving them a head start on the Vero rewards system.

Can I refer myself?

No. Self-referrals are not allowed and will be automatically detected and rejected.

What happens if a referral is suspected of fraud?

Payouts undergo a manual review period. Any suspicious activity (e.g. fake accounts) will be flagged and the payout will be cancelled.
